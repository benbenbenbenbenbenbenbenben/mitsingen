About latency

Latency is a magic number that adjusts for slight delays in how your computer deals with sound. It changes how your recorded voice is lined up with the pre-recorded parts when you "Review".

If you feel like your voice is always coming after the pre-recorded part (and it isn't because you're actually singing late!) try increasing this number. Increase in small steps, and it is unlikely to ever be more than 0.5.

If you feel like your voice is always coming before the pre-recorded part (and it isn't because you're actually singing early!) try decreasing this number.
